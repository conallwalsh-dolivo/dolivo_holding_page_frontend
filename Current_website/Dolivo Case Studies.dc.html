OPERATIONAL ASSET · IRELAND, 2×100 MW SOLAR

Eliminating six-figure grid fines

CUSTOMER

Renewable asset owner operating two large utility-scale solar farms.

CHALLENGE

Two sites faced multi-six-figure annual grid fines tied to import capacity and reactive power. The customer needed the root cause, and whether controls or contracted capacity could fix it.

DOLIVO'S WORK

We built an analysis pipeline from SCADA data and power system models, isolating the sustained operating behaviour driving the exposure from short-duration events.

RECOMMENDATIONS
  • Revised import capacity based on actual site behaviour
  • Control-system changes to reduce overnight reactive load
  • Inverter rotation strategy to manage equipment stress
  • Fixed capacity costs balanced against avoided fines

OPERATIONAL ASSET · MAINE, 2×6 MW SOLAR

Recovering 3% energy production through Volt-VAR realignment

CUSTOMER

Utility-scale solar developer and operator, solar sites in Maine, US, 6 MW export limits.

CHALLENGE

Elevated voltage at the point of interconnection pushed both sites into exporting excess reactive power. The Volt-VAR curve set at commissioning no longer matched actual grid conditions.

DOLIVO'S WORK

We assessed interconnection voltage, reactive power behaviour and inverter response to find the gap between expected and actual conditions.

RECOMMENDATIONS

A revised Volt-VAR curve aligned to actual grid conditions, restoring supportive reactive power behaviour.
