WHERE WE SUPPORT
01 · Development & acquisition

03 · Commissioning & handover

CORE AREAS OF WORK
Technical due diligence

Independent review of grid connection, electrical design and control systems for material technical risk.

Grid compliance & model alignment

Checking that models, equipment and site settings stay consistent with current design and grid requirements.

Control-system & site optimisation

Assessing controller behaviour, inverter settings and transformer configuration for compliance and performance gains.

Operational diagnostics

Using SCADA data and power system models to find the root cause of fines, lost generation and abnormal behaviour.

An initial engagement typically focuses on a single project or one or more operational assets.
